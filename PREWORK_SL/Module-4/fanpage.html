Bio

Ed Sheeran is an English singer, songwriter, record producer, actor and is one of the world 's best selling music artists and won multiple awards for his music. He has sold more than 150 million records worldwide. The genres of his music include pop, hip-hop, and folk-pop. Ed Sheeran was born on 17 February 1991 in Halifax, West Yorkshire, UK and started showing interest in music from quite a young age. At an early age, Ed started playing guitar as well singing in the local church. From his teenage days, he had started recording CDs and selling them. At the age of 14, he released "The Orange Room" and also "Spinning Man", both regarded as important works of his early years. In 2012, he won the Brits wards for Best British Male Solo Artist and British Breakthrough Act of the Year. His songs slowly started gaining more popularity and were widely appreciated by the public.
